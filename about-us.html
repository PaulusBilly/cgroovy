About C'Groovy

Our History

C'Groovy was founded in 2022 by a group of passionate music lovers who believed in the power of music to connect people across the globe. What started as a small project in a college dorm room quickly grew into a thriving platform, bringing together artists and listeners from all walks of life.

Our Journey

2022: C'Groovy is conceptualized and the first prototype is launched.
2023: The platform gains its first 10,000 users and partners with indie artists.
2024: C'Groovy expands globally, introducing curated playlists and live sessions.
2025: Now a leading music streaming service, C'Groovy continues to innovate and inspire music lovers everywhere.
